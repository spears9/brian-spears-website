About Brian Spears

Brian Spears is a renowned physicist and innovator known for his groundbreaking work in fusion energy research, high-performance computing, and the integration of artificial intelligence into scientific simulation. With over two decades of experience at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL), he has led pivotal projects—from advancing inertial confinement fusion (ICF) experiments to pioneering cognitive simulation strategies that merge AI with physics.

His visionary leadership has propelled initiatives such as the National Ignition Facility’s fusion ignition experiments and the creation of the AI Innovation Incubator (AI3), fostering collaborations with industry giants like Google, IBM, and NVIDIA. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4} :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
